Our Success Story

Where we are...

. . .and where we came from!.

From running the internship wholly on slack to assigning tasks and grading them, managing the HNG Internship program was a cumbersome task. The management of the program deemed it wise to develop an app that will lighten the burden. HNGM was born: an app to facilitate easier mentor-mentee integration thereby reducing the challenges that were hitherto faced by both interns and mentors. The app aims at providing a platform for interns to easily access their task, view their progress, see the mentors assigned to them and have ready submission links for any given task. It also makes it easier for mentors to see the interns assigned to them, grade them easily and give much-needed feedback to the interns.
